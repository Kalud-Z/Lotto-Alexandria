The Hot-Numbers Tip

With the Hot Numbers tip, a combination per playing field is shown from the twentieth most true numbers of all Aligen draws.

The Cold-Numbers-Tip

With the Cold Numbers tip, a combination per playing field is selected from the twenty least drawn numbers of all previous draws.

Your Lucky-Day Tip

With the lucky day tip, numbers are selected from the current date and supplemented with random numbers.

The Prime Numbers Tip

With the prime number tip, a number combination is selected for each playing field, which consists of the prime numbers.
